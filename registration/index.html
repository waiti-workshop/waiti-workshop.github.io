Workshop Registration

If you are interested in attending, please check off the appropriate box on the conference registration form and add in the AI for cyber Threat Intelligence (WAITI) Workshop fee (More details here: https://www.acsac.org/2026/registration/). If you want to attend, please check the appropriate box on the conference registration form and add the AI for Cyber Threat Intelligence (WAITI) Workshop fee. For accepted papers, at least one author must register and attend. In-person attendance is strongly encouraged. Remote presentations are not normally permitted but may be approved by the Program Committee on a case-by-case basis under exceptional circumstances.
